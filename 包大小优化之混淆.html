开启proguard代码优化


1. build.gradle中设置 proguardFiles getDefaultProguardFile('proguard-android-optimize.txt')
2. proguard-rules.pro中开启
-optimizations !code/simplification/arithmetic,!code/simplification/cast,!field/*,!class/merging/*
-optimizationpasses 5
3. proguard-rules.pro中关闭
#-keepattributes SourceFile,LineNumberTable 4. 每个module中加入proguard-rules.pro，build.gradle中开启代码混淆
